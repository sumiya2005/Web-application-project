Mandala
>
Mandala paintings are intricate, circular designs symbolizing the universe in Hindu and Buddhist cultures. The term "mandala," meaning "circle" in Sanskrit, represents wholeness and harmony. Used in meditation and rituals, they feature recurring motifs and vibrant colors that promote balance. Creating or coloring mandalas fosters relaxation and self-expression, blending creativity and spirituality.
